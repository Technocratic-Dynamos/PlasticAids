About the project

Our MakeItHappen project aims at preserving fossil fuels by reducing the production of petrol. In this project we will focus on two of the most significant problems we have: Preservation of fossil fuels and reduction of plastic and plastic pollution. Our project is eco friendly and efficient. It is affordable too. About 30% of the world population (people below line of poverty) will benefit, as the stock of fossil fuels are reducing ,the price of petrol is exceeding limits with it being highly unaffordable. Our project will make petrol more accessible and cheaper.

What is Plastic Pollution?

Plastic pollution is the accumulation of plastic objects and particles in the Earth's environment that adversely affects wildlife, wildlife habitat, and humans. Plastics that act as pollutants are categorized into micro-, meso-, or macro debris, based on size.

How it works?

Through a series of processes we will convert plastic to fuel that can be further distilled to petrol

The processes are quite simple. The two processes that we are using today are evaporation and condensation. Now you might be thinking how evaporation with solids. Well not directly with solid plastic but with the liquid plastic that is acquired after burning plastic.
